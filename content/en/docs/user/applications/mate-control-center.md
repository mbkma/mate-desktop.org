The MATE Control Center is the central place to configure your desktop. It provides a unified launcher for all system and personal preference panels, grouped by category.

Open it from **System → Control Center** in the panel menu.

![Control Center window](/images/applications/mate-control-center-window.png "MATE Control Center")

## Categories

### Personal

- **About Me** — user name, contact info, and profile photo
- **Preferred Applications** — default browser, email client, and terminal
- **Startup Applications** — programs that launch automatically at login

### Look and Feel

- **Appearance** — GTK theme, window borders, icons, and fonts
- **Screensaver** — idle time, screensaver selection, and lock settings
- **Windows** — window behavior, focus mode, and compositing

### Hardware

- **Displays** — monitor resolution, orientation, and multi-monitor layout
- **Keyboard** — layout, repeat rate, and accessibility settings
- **Mouse** — pointer speed, button configuration, and touchpad options
- **Sound** — volume levels and sound theme
- **Power Management** — battery, display sleep, and suspend settings

### Administration

- **Time and Date** — system clock and timezone
- **Users and Groups** — manage local user accounts
